Breez SDK

Lightning-as-a-Service

The Breez SDK provides developers with an end-to-end solution for integrating self-custodial Lightning payments into their apps and services, eliminating the need for third parties and simplifying the complexities of Bitcoin and Lightning.


With just a few lines of code, developers can seamlessly onboard billions of users to the future of peer-to-peer payments. From social apps integrating tipping between users, to content creation apps adding Bitcoin monetization, or crypto wallets incorporating self-custodial payments — the potential use cases of the Breez SDK are endless.
