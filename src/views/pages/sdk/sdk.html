Breez SDK

Make Value Move Like Information

The Breez SDK is the best-in-class solution for developers adding instant, non-custodial bitcoin to their apps and services, removing third-party dependencies and enabling value to move as easily as information.


With just a few lines of code, onboard billions of users to Bitcoin: an open, decentralized, and neutral monetary network. From social platforms adding tipping, to content apps embedding payments, to crypto wallets offering instant bitcoin and stablecoins. Wherever value needs to move, the Breez SDK gets it there.
