Bitcoin Payments

From Digital Gold to Everyday Currency

Breez and 1A1z have unveiled a new report, Bitcoin Payments: From Digital Gold to Everyday Currency, exploring how bitcoin has evolved from a store of value into an everyday medium of exchange. The report examines real-world adoption, revealing over 650 million people now have access to bitcoin payments, with case studies from global businesses like Pick n Pay (Africa), Mercari (Japan), and Mondo Convenienza (Italy).
