Privacy Policy for Glow: Lightning Fast Bitcoin Mobile App

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Glow: Lightning Fast Bitcoin ("Glow", "our app") is a self-custodial bitcoin wallet developed by Breez Development LTD ("Breez", "we"). We do not collect, store, or sell personal data, and Glow contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind. Your wallet keys are created and stored on your device; Breez never has access to them or to your funds. This policy explains what information Glow handles, and what never leaves your device.

Your wallet stays on your device

  • Your recovery phrase (seed) is generated on your device and stored in your device's secure storage: the iOS Keychain protected by Face ID or Touch ID, or the Android Keystore protected by your fingerprint or face unlock. It is never transmitted to Breez.
  • If you create your wallet with a passkey, the passkey is managed by your platform account (for example iCloud Keychain or Google Password Manager) under that provider's terms. Your biometric data never leaves your device and is never visible to Glow or Breez.
  • Contacts, payment labels, and app settings are stored only on your device.
  • Diagnostic logs stay on your device and are shared only if you explicitly choose "Share Logs".
  • Because your keys never reach us, we cannot recover them for you if they are lost; keep your recovery phrase or passkey safe.

Network communication

To send and receive payments, our app communicates with:

  • Lightning and Spark network infrastructure operated by Breez and its service providers, including Lightspark and Flashnet, which process the payment data needed to route your transactions (invoices, amounts, and node identifiers).
  • Public block-explorer services, such as Blockstream's Esplora or mempool.space, to look up deposit addresses, confirmations, and fee estimates.
  • The server behind a Lightning address or LNURL: paying user@domain.com contacts that domain directly to resolve the payment details.
  • Breez's exchange-rate service, to display amounts in your local currency.

Like any internet service, these servers see your IP address as a technical necessity, along with the data each lookup requires (queried addresses for block explorers, the payment request for Lightning address and LNURL servers). This data is used only to provide the service: we do not build profiles, we do not link it to your identity, and we do not sell it. Where these services are operated by third parties, their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies. Bitcoin and Lightning transactions are public by design on their respective networks. Because we do not store this data, we are also unable to produce it for anyone else.

App permissions

  • Camera: used only to scan QR codes. Frames are processed on your device and are never stored or uploaded.
  • Face ID / biometric unlock: used only to unlock the app's secure storage. As noted above, biometric data is handled by your device's operating system and never leaves it.

Other Websites and Third-Party Services

If you buy bitcoin in our app, you are redirected to a third-party provider (such as MoonPay or Cash App). The purchase happens on their platform under their own privacy policy and identity checks; Glow only provides the receiving address for your wallet.

Children

Glow is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly handle their data.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We will update this page if the policy changes, and note material changes in app release notes.

Further Information

If you have any questions on our privacy policies or wish to report a complaint, please contact us at contact@breez.technology. If you contact us, we receive the information you choose to send (such as your email address) and use it only to respond.
